# Location: Frozen Lake

## Rumors
- North of Myeve, near a mountain, there is a frozen body of water where dwarves and kobolds have been seen digging in the ice together. [[2022-02-02 Session 1]]
- Something beneath the ice can bend minds. [[2022-02-02 Session 1]]
- Those working there may not be acting of their own free will. [[2022-02-02 Session 1]]

## What is Known
- Frozen Lake is a body of frozen water north of Myeve near a mountain. [[2022-02-02 Session 1]]
- Dwarves and kobolds were both present there, working together to dig through the ice. [[2022-02-02 Session 1]]
- A giant three-eyed creature was seen beneath the ice. [[2022-02-02 Session 1]]
- The creature was later thought to likely be an aboleth. [[2022-02-02 Session 1]]
- Those working at the site appeared weak and damaged. [[2022-02-02 Session 1]]
- Their feet showed signs of frost burn. [[2022-02-02 Session 1]]
- It seemed they may not have wanted to fight, but were unable to resist the will of the thing beneath the ice. [[2022-02-02 Session 1]]

## History
- During the events of the 2022-02-02 Session 1 report, the party traveled from Myeve to investigate reports of dwarves and kobolds digging together in the frozen lake. [[2022-02-02 Session 1]]
- When members of the party approached, something beneath the ice attempted to control their minds. [[2022-02-02 Session 1]]
- A fight broke out with the dwarves and kobolds at the site. [[2022-02-02 Session 1]]
- After the conflict, the party captured one kobold and returned to town with the prisoner. [[2022-02-02 Session 1]]
- John Harrison later suggested the creature beneath the ice was likely an aboleth. [[2022-02-02 Session 1]]
