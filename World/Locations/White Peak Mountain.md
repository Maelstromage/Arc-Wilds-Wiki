# Location: White Peak Mountain

## Rumors
- White Peak Mountain is a harsh mountain region of blizzards, hidden caves, and ancient things trapped in ice. [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
- A frost giant lies frozen somewhere upon the mountain. [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
- Deep within the mountain, even stranger things remain locked in the ice. [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]

## What is Known
- White Peak Mountain is a mountain location reached by climbing upward through worsening cold and snow. [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
- Mir Batola is the old name for this place.
- A blizzard was building on the mountain during the events of the report. [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
- A frost giant was found frozen in the ice there. [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
- An avalanche struck as the frost giant began to break free. [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
- The party found a cave in the mountain. [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
- Inside, there is a cavern and the back wall was melting. [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
- A purple worm was trapped in ice within the mountain. [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
- Dwarves and a chest were also found locked in the ice. [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
- Deeper inside was an entrance into a small room and, beyond a wall, a chained ogre zombie. [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
- Ogra, leader of the orcish clans, was encountered there. [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]

## History
- During the events of the 2022-03-02 Session 5 report, the party turned back from the ruined dwarf settlement and chose to pursue the dragon into the mountain. [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
- On the climb, they encountered signs of greater buried dangers: the frozen frost giant, avalanche conditions, and the frozen remains of stranger creatures. [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
- Their path led them into the mountain interior, where they discovered caves, trapped creatures, imprisoned dwarves, and the beginnings of an orc-held space. [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
