# Location: The Party's Land

## Rumors
- The party has acquired land outside town and means to build something substantial there.
- Work has already begun on a future stronghold, and some say a wizard's tower will be part of it.

## What is Known
- The Party's Land is an eight-acre plot acquired through the party's dealings in town.
- The land was secured through a bargain involving Zarya and a matchmaking arrangement.
- The party intends to build a bastion there combining a stronghold and a wizard's tower.
- Builders had already begun breaking ground there shortly after the land changed hands.
- The site became the place where the psychopomp's ankh was used to open a gateway and guide lingering spirits toward the afterlife.

## History
- After returning from Bone Speaker Ruins, the party arranged to receive the land through Zarya.
- They invested gold into construction and planning for a future base of operations.
- On 2026-03-11, the land became the site of the party's first deliberate opening of a gateway to the Gray Realm so trapped spirits could pass on.
