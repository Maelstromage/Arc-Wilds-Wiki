# Location: Carceri

## Rumors
- Carceri is a prison plane from which few, if any, escape.
- Some call it Tartarus.
- The ground itself can kill the unwary.
- Those who go there do not return unchanged.
- The very land seems hostile to mortal life.
- Some whisper of a confessional threshold within the plane that strips denial away before deeper passage is allowed.

# DM Notes
## Hidden Truths
- Carceri is composed of six known layers.
- Travel deeper into Carceri should feel like descent into harsher and more absolute forms of imprisonment.
- The plane should feel less like a single landscape and more like a chain of prison-realms.
- The Arch of Justice exists as part of the plane's prison logic, forcing self-revelation before deeper confinement.

## NPC Truths
- Kronos is not merely a landmark; he is part of the logic of punishment and containment that defines the plane.

## Mechanics / Encounters
- Each deeper layer should feel more difficult to survive, traverse, or understand than the last.
- Navigation, safety, and escape should become harder as the party descends.
- The environment itself should remain one of the primary threats.
- Blackstone thresholds, confession gates, and imprisoned divine beings should reinforce the plane's prison logic.

## Future Hooks
### Orthrys
- Use as the first and most immediate face of Carceri’s prison nature.
- Best suited for the party’s first impressions: lethal terrain, despair, and the realization that escape is difficult.

### Cathrys
- Use as a harsher and more oppressive layer beyond Orthrys.
- Suited to broken structures, failed hopes, and signs of deeper confinement.

### Minethys
- Use for deprivation, exposure, and hostile travel.
- Good for pursuit, attrition, and long-haul survival pressure.

### Colothys
- Use for stronger threats, hidden enclaves, and difficult passage.
- Good for factions, ambushes, and beings that have adapted to imprisonment.

### Porphatys
- Use for stranger, older, and more mythically dangerous material.
- Good for ancient prisoners, cosmic punishments, and frightening revelations.

### Agathys
- Use as the deepest and most severe layer.
- Best reserved for major revelations, impossible prisons, and entities that should not be reached lightly.
