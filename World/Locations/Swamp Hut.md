# Location: Swamp Hut

## Rumors
- There is a hut on a swamp island that can only be reached safely by a single boat and a lamppost-marked approach.
- Tentacles reach through the windows and snatch animals from outside.
- An old woman lives there, though some claim they have seen more than one.
- Flumphs drift in the waters nearby.
- The hut lies within [[Region Black Tooth Marshes]].
- Lizardfolk fled the area because of something strange and unnatural near the hut.
- Some say the hut's mistress can answer questions about death, spirits, and things beyond the grave.

## What is Known
- The hut stands on a small island in the swamp.
- Reaching it by swimming is dangerous because of the nearby flumphs and the hazards of the water.
- Strange tentacles have been seen reaching from the hut or from around its cauldron to snatch living things.
- The place feels unnatural and wrong.
- There is some kind of presence, force, or phenomenon tied to the hut that has driven others away from the area.
- As far as most people know, the hut is associated with an old woman living in eerie isolation.
- Black Tooth lizardfolk once lived in the surrounding marsh, but later fled the region.
- The party later visited the hut seeking answers about a necromantic ankh and the burden of escorting spirits.
- An old woman there communed through the cauldron and led the party to an answer about opening a gateway for the dead.

## History
- The lizardfolk of the area fled because of the presence surrounding the hut.
- Stories about the place have spread through the region over time, especially tales of tentacles, strange lights, and the old woman in the swamp.
- At least one recent account described an old woman communing with something through the cauldron.
- On 2026-03-11, the party journeyed to the hut for guidance on how to help spirits move on after becoming entangled with a psychopomp's ankh.

## People
### Old Woman
- A cryptic swamp-dweller who stirred the cauldron and encouraged the party to seek answers through direct contact with what lived within it

## Loose Threads
- What is really in or beneath the hut?
- What is the old woman actually doing there?
- Why did the lizardfolk flee?
- What is the true nature of the cauldron and the tentacles?
- Is there a way to stop or seal whatever is happening there?
- What exactly is the relationship between the hut, the old woman, and the realm of the dead?

# DM Notes
## Hidden Truths
- The lizardfolk fled because of the Old One's presence in the marsh.
- The hut is tied to a portal connected to an Old God or eldritch entity older than the gods of the world.
- The hags and a wizard created the portal.
- In doing so, they accidentally created kuo-toa, who now have a shanty town somewhere in the region.
- On 2026-03-11, an old woman was confirmed to be communing with the Old God through the cauldron and its tentacle manifestations.
- That contact granted knowledge in a non-verbal, experiential way and produced a feeling of unity with a vast entity.
- The cauldron communion revealed how the psychopomp's ankh could open a gateway between the living world and the realm of the dead.

## NPC Truths
- K'Lgnk, also rendered in notes as Kulingnick, is the nothic tied to the hut.
- The "old woman" is one of three hags associated with the hut: [[Rebecca]], [[Sandy]], and [[Beatrice]].
- The hags are not always present.
- They are not necessarily immediately hostile and may be neutral, negotiable, or opportunistic depending on circumstances.
- At least one of the hags values or enjoys the portal.
- [[K'lgnk]], a nothic, is sometimes present in the hut.
- K'lgnk is secretive, unstable, childlike or playful at times, and may turn hostile.
- He knows hidden truths or player secrets.
- He may once have been a wizard caught up in the portal's influence.
- [[Crohl Ba'an]] is a two-headed ogre wizard or oni tied to the Old God's power.

## Mechanics / Encounters
- Flumphs are part of the local danger around the hut.
- The hut contains traps and healing potions.
- There is a ritual capable of closing the portal.
- Reaching the hut by swimming is especially dangerous due to the flumphs and surrounding hazards.
- The cauldron's manifestations can physically reach outward and seize living creatures.
- There is a circle of zombie corpses somewhere in the surrounding swamp.

## Future Hooks
- The swamp hut is only one site within the larger Black Tooth Marshes region.
- The Old God wants Chrol Ba'an dead so that the power placed in him may return and the entity may be released fully into the world.
- If released, that return would bring one thousand years of darkness.
- Chrol Ba'an was last known to be in the Cloven Hoof Cave.
- The full purpose and function of the portal remain unresolved.
- The full extent of the Old God's influence is still unknown.
- The hut may remain one of the few places where answers about death, souls, and the afterlife can be sought.
