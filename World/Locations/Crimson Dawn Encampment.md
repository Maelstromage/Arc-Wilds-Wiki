# Location: Crimson Dawn Encampment

## Rumors
- After Adenizeal fell, the Crimson Dawn continued their crusade from a new encampment.
- The encampment is tied to mining operations.
- Flyers in Odin’s Eye ask for help against pirate attacks in the area.
- Regular shipments move through the nearby waters.

## What is Known
- Crimson Dawn Encampment is a settlement and mining site run by the Crimson Dawn.
- It was established after Adenizeal fell.
- The faction uses the site as a base to continue its crusade.
- The encampment depends on regular shipments moving through nearby waters.
- Travelers to the area may encounter officers of the settlement.
- The encampment is in conflict with pirates operating nearby.

## History
- After the fall of Adenizeal, a faction called the Crimson Dawn chose to carry on its cause.
- In time, they established an encampment and mining operation of their own.
- Their expansion brought them into conflict with pirates and with the region’s older inhabitants.

# DM Notes
## Hidden Truths
- People are tricked into becoming citizens of the settlement.
- Noncompliant citizens usually become prisoners in the mines.
- Goblins used to run the place before the Crimson Dawn took it over.
- Some prisoners escaped the mines and are now attacking the settlement.

## Mechanics / Encounters
- If the party travels to the area, they can encounter two officers of the settlement and learn the local situation.
- The Crimson Dawn offers to make the party officers with steady pay if they capture the pirates and turn them in.
- Crimson Dawn has regular shipments travelling through the waters of the area.
- Pirates had boarded up the cave from the inside because of goblin attacks.
