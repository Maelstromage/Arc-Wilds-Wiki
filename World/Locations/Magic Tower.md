# Location: Magic Tower

## Rumors
- A wizard's tower once stood on the outskirts of town but now hangs inside a cloud high above the ground.
- Those who have looked into it speak of bizarre, dangerous floors and strange magical tests.
- Some say the wizard inside is still alive because the tower continues to move and persist.

## What is Known
- Magic Tower is a wizard's tower on the outskirts of town.
- It no longer rests on the ground where it once stood.
- The tower now floats within a dense, stationary cloud high above the land, casting a shadow below.
- The original ground site remains as a cleared circular plot with a cobblestone path and the faint impression of a foundation.
- The tower appears to contain multiple dangerous and unusual floors.
- Jeremy's scouting revealed a life-size chessboard, a flooded floor with sharks, a locked chamber of glowing vials and goblets, a suit of armor that mirrored intruders, a room filled with squirrels, and a congregation of thirteen masked figures in black robes.
- Reaching the top likely requires surviving every floor in sequence.

## History
- The party traveled to the tower site after John Harrison directed them there to learn more about a growing magical quartz cluster.
- On arrival, they discovered the tower had risen into the sky and was now enclosed within a cloud.
- A nearby farmer confirmed the tower had been there for some time.
- The party sent Jeremy to scout the interior and decided the tower was too dangerous to assault at that time.
