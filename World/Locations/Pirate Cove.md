# Location: Pirate Cove

## Rumors
- A pirate hideout lies hidden behind a giant waterfall.
- Greybeard’s pirates are said to be hiding there.
- They are pressured by goblins and hobgoblins and have boarded parts of the place up to keep them out.
- The cove has multiple entrances, including underwater tunnels.
- There is said to be a pirate mole in town feeding them shipment information.

## What is Known
- Pirate Cove is hidden behind a giant waterfall.
- It serves as a hideout for pirates.
- The pirates there are associated with Greybeard’s pirates.
- The cove includes tunnels and underwater tunnels.
- It has multiple entrances.
- Some areas have been boarded up because of pressure from goblins in the area.
- The pirates are willing to make deals with adventurers if they are helped and if the location is kept secret.

## History
- Pirate Cove became a hidden refuge for Greybeard’s pirates behind the waterfall.
- Over time, the hideout came under pressure from goblins and hobgoblins in the surrounding area.
- The pirates adapted by fortifying and boarding up sections of the cove.

# DM Notes
## Hidden Truths
- The pirates have a concrete plan to infiltrate the city, get aboard a supply boat, and take it over.
- They do not want to destroy the boat, because they hope to rob it again later.
- Their long-term goal is to get rich enough to defeat the Rebellion of Crimson Dawn.
