# Location: Black Dragon Temple

## Rumors
- An underground temple lies somewhere in Black Tooth Marshes.
- A strange door there can speak.
- There is also a talking tree connected to the site.
- Kobold followers are said to gather there.

## What is Known
- Black Dragon Temple is an underground temple within [[Region Black Tooth Marshes]].
- The temple is associated with Black Tooth Marshes and the Black Tooth lizardfolk naming of the region.
- The site includes a door that talks.
- There is also a talking tree tied to the temple.
- Kobold followers are present there.

## History
- The temple stands as one of the deeper and older sites within Black Tooth Marshes.
- It is distinct from the swamp hut, though likely part of the same broader web of danger in the marsh.
