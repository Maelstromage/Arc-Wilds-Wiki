# Wandering Encounter: Wandering Djinn

## What It Is
- A djinn associated with a jar and a scattered pile of belongings.

## What is Known
- The encounter involves a pile of things lying around and a jar containing a djinn.
- The djinn was once trapped in the jar.
- If released, it is dangerous.

## Behavior
- The djinn does not like humanity.
- Its anger comes from having been locked in a jar.
- Once freed, it intends to wreak havoc on people.

## People
### Djinn
- Formerly trapped in a jar

## Loose Threads
- Who trapped the djinn in the jar?
- How long was it imprisoned?
- Can it be reasoned with, or only unleashed?
- What destruction does it plan to bring once freed?

# DM Notes
## Hidden Truths
- The djinn’s hatred of humanity is specifically rooted in its imprisonment.

## NPC / Creature Truths
- The djinn is not grateful for release; freedom turns immediately toward vengeance.

## Mechanics / Encounters
- The encounter centers on a jar-bound djinn and the consequences of releasing it.

## Future Hooks
- towns harmed by the freed djinn
- the identity of the one who imprisoned it
- the possibility of binding it again
