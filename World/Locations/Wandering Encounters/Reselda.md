# Wandering Encounter: Reselda

## What It Is
- A succubus with two panthers, encountered in a brightly colored tent.

## What is Known
- Reselda is a succubus.
- She keeps two panthers with her.
- Their names are Inka and Jasmin.
- She stays in a brightly colored tent.
- She has a hookah.

## Behavior
- Reselda is tied to devilish bargains, packages, and pursuit.
- She seeks to capture a tiefling family connected to a book of devil names.
- She also wants the little girl Quinn taken to Dispater.

## People
### Reselda
- Succubus

### Inka
- Panther companion

### Jasmin
- Panther companion

### Quinn
- Little girl sought by Reselda

## Loose Threads
- Why does Reselda want Quinn taken to Dispater?
- What is in the package and why is the command word important?
- What tie does Reselda have to the tiefling family and the devil-name book?
- Why is the key from the Tomb of Horrors tied to the box?

# DM Notes
## Hidden Truths
- The command word tied to the package is "Dispater."
- Reselda wants a tiefling family captured because they possess a book with devil names in it.
- The tiefling family has a spell that prevents people from finding them and hides the book.
- Reselda wants Quinn specifically taken to Dispater.
- The box can only be destroyed by a key.
- That key was in the Tomb of Horrors.

## NPC / Creature Truths
- Inka and Jasmin are Reselda's panthers.

## Mechanics / Encounters
- A meeting with Reselda likely centers on bargains, pursuit, capture, and devil-linked errands.

## Future Hooks
- Quinn’s fate
- the tiefling family with the book of devil names
- Dispater’s involvement
- the missing key from the Tomb of Horrors
