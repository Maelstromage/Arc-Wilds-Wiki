# Wandering Encounter: Maelistor

## What It Is
- A tiefling family of devil summoners.

## What is Known
- Maelistor is a wandering family group rather than a fixed settlement.
- They are tieflings.
- The family includes:
  - Laxtor, the father
  - Dennifer, the mother
  - Quinn, the daughter

## Behavior
- They are tied to devil summoning.
- An encounter with them is likely to involve dangerous ritual magic and dealings with devils.

## People
### Laxtor
- Father of the family

### Dennifer
- Mother of the family

### Quinn
- Daughter of the family

## Loose Threads
- Where did they learn devil summoning?
- Who are they trying to control or bargain with next?
- What does Quinn’s future role in the family look like?
- How dangerous are they if crossed?

# DM Notes
## Hidden Truths
- They possess a book containing the true names of devils they control.

## NPC / Creature Truths
- Their power over devils depends on knowing and using those true names.

## Mechanics / Encounters
- They can perform a ritual using blood sacrifices to summon devils whose names they do not yet know.

## Future Hooks
- The family may become recurring summoners, rivals, or unstable allies.
- Their book of true names could become a major object of interest.
