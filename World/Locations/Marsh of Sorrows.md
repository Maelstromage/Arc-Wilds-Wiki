# Location: Marsh of Sorrows

## Rumors
- The Marsh of Sorrows lives up to its name.
- Those who enter it may find themselves overcome by sadness.
- Strange figures, broken shelters, and haunted dealings lie somewhere within.
- Travelers speak of a place where it is hard to tell whether sorrow is in the land or in the soul.

## What is Known
- Marsh of Sorrows is a marsh region marked by emotional and supernatural oppression.
- Travelers there may need to withstand waves of sadness. 
- The party traveled there after being pointed in that direction by the efreeti. [[2021-10-28]]
- The party used a folding boat in the marsh. [[2021-10-28]]
- A depressed turtle was encountered there. [[2021-10-28]]
- There was a shed or hut in the marsh. [[2021-10-28]]
- Ragred lived there and had a hippogriff named Beakbuck outside. [[2021-10-28]]
- Ragred later fled on Beakbuck. [[2021-10-28]]
- A little girl was found hiding in a room there. [[2021-10-28]]
- The girl spoke only Toki Pona. [[2021-10-28]]
- The party learned that she had two souls. [[2021-10-28]]

## History
- During the events recorded in the 2021-10-28 session recap, the party traveled from the carnival thread into the Marsh of Sorrows. [[2021-10-28]]
- The marsh became the setting for the confrontation around BooNazEE, Ragred, Madam Gravor, and the little girl with two souls. [[2021-10-28]]
- The party survived the struggle there and carried the consequences of it back out of the marsh. [[2021-10-28]]

# DM Notes
## Hidden Truths
- BooNazEE was central to the soul-conflict in the marsh.
- Madam Gravor contested BooNazEE over souls there.
- BooNazEE emerged as a banshee.
- All had given in to the sadness of the swamp.

## NPC Truths
- Ragred’s hut was blown up from summoning Gravor.
- Ragred escaped on the hippogriff Beakbuck.
- Madam Gravor summoned a zombie beholder.
- A bone claw was also summoned into the struggle.

## Mechanics / Encounters
- Travelers may need to roll a Constitution save or be overcome by sadness.
- The party could still track invisible movement there because of tracks.
- BooNazEE, Madam Gravor, and the summoned creatures created a multi-sided conflict over souls.
- The ring given to the little girl allowed her to perform resurrections.
