# Zarya

- Drow woman
- White hair
- About 125 years old
- Appears to be in her mid-20s
- Owner of Zarya's General Store in Myeve
- Shopkeeper and merchant
- Confident
- Composed
- Socially sharp
- Speaks directly
- Efficient in conversation and business
- Attractive and self-assured
- Keeps a well-stocked general store
- Sells practical supplies for adventurers and travelers
- Prices are fair, but not generous
- Pays attention to what customers buy and what they skip
- Upsells intelligently when it benefits her
- Owns land
- Wants to build something permanent
- Has said she plans to build when she finds a strong husband

- Was intrigued by the party's description of a rugged, emotionally vulnerable farmer as a potential match
