# Beatrice

- Hag associated with the Swamp Hut
- One of three hags tied to the hut
- Not always present
- Neutral or negotiable rather than immediately hostile
- Opportunistic
- May have her own view of the portal and what should be done about it
- Older notes spell the name as Beatice in one place
