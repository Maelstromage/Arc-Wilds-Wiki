# Frostmaw

- White dragon
- Wanted the treasure within Bone Speaker Ruins
- Green-scale kobolds were investigating how to enter the temple on Frostmaw's behalf
- Flew over the region during the events tied to the ruins
- Tied to the danger and pressure surrounding the site
- Confronted the party after they left the dwarf village [[2022-02-23 Session 4]]
- Demanded they throw down their loot [[2022-02-23 Session 4]]
- Nearly killed them before being convinced they would serve as his minions [[2022-02-23 Session 4]]
- Was strongly aware of the smell of dwarves on them [[2022-02-23 Session 4]]
- Returned to the party after being attacked from stealth [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
- Unleashed a breath attack that dropped four people [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
- Was driven back toward his hoard by an illusion of a red dragon [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
- Flew toward the dwarven settlement as the storm worsened [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
- Was responsible for the destruction of the dwarf settlement before the party could return [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
